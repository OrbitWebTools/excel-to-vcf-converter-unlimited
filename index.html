Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?
Yes. This tool operates entirely on the client-side. This means the conversion happens inside your web browser using your device's memory. Your contact list is never uploaded to any server or database.

How do I fix phone numbers showing as 9.87E+09?
Excel often converts long phone numbers into scientific notation. You do not need to fix this manually. Our converter algorithm automatically detects and expands scientific notation back into normal phone numbers during the conversion process.
