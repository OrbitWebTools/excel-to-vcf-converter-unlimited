# Unlimited Excel to VCF Converter (Free & Secure)

The only **Excel to VCF Converter** that runs 100% offline in your browser. Convert unlimited contacts from Excel (.xlsx/.xls) to vCard (.vcf) format for free.

## Why use this tool?
- **Unlimited Rows:** Convert 10,000+ contacts in seconds.
- **Privacy Guaranteed:** No file upload. Runs locally on your device.
- **iPhone & Android Support:** Uses VCF 3.0 standard with UTF-8 encoding.
- **Scientific Notation Fix:** Automatically fixes phone numbers formatted like `9.87E+09`.

## How to use:
1. Upload your Excel file.
2. Select the "Name" and "Phone" columns.
3. Click Convert and Download.
